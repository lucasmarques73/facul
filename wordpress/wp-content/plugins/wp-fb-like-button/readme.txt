Adds the Facebook universal like button to your posts

== Description ==

With the Wordpress Facebook "Like" Button Plugin by Mind Development & Design
LLC , you can add a Facebook "Like" button to your posts. Simply choose an
option from the options menu, or insert <!--fb_like--> into your post.

You may also use `<?php insert_wp_fb_like(); ?>` in your templates to display
the button anywhere in your template.

== Installation ==

1. Download the plugin.
1. Unzip the compressed file to your wp-content/plugins folder.
1. Browse to the Plugins page under wp-admin and activate the plugin.
1. Setting the options: Browse to Settings->WP FB Like Button.
